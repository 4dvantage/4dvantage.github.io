Research

While classic robotic automation excels at handling predictable rigid objects like boxes and automotive parts, it cannot effectively manage non-rigid materials such as fabric due to their complex behavior. We have been developing modular and interpretable robotic models that can generalize to messy and chaotic states.

Introducing Super Series®, a set of modular and interpretable capabilities for linen services, starting with SuperSort® and SuperPick®. These solutions demonstrate an economically viable solution for a variety of tasks.
Our SuperSort® system processes soiled linens. This single workcell can amplify human productivity and integrates with existing operations across flexible and modular capabilities.
