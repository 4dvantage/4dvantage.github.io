Research

Sufficiently Advanced is building productive AI into the world. We are engineers, scientists, and hackers that build value. While classic robotic automation excels at handling predictable rigid objects like boxes and automotive parts, it cannot effectively manage non-rigid materials such as fabric due to their complex behavior, leaving major industries reliant on manual or outdated processes. We have been developing modular and interpretable robotic models that can generalize to messy and chaotic states.
